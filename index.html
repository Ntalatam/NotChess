        v1.3 Field Guide

        How To Play

        1

        Your Turn

        Draw up to four cards, then make one legal chess move. You may play one chaos card before that move.

        2

        Moving

        Click one of your pieces, then click a glowing target square. Promotion choices appear beside the board.

        3

        Cards

        Click a face-up card to play it. If it needs targets, legal squares flash; press Escape to cancel.

        4

        Mutations

        Captures can mutate the attacker. Hover colored piece badges to see powers like Shielded, Volatile, or Wild.

        5

        Tiles

        Animated board tiles change movement: Lava and Void destroy, Ice slides, Portals warp, Mines explode.

        6

        Winning

        Checkmate still wins. Kings can also be destroyed by Wacko effects; simultaneous King destruction is a draw.
